\clearpage\section{State of the art of the technology used or applied in this thesis:}

 With the boom of AI that is currently on-going, increased the offer of automated tools to generate hardware. These tools could be broken down into two main groups. The ones based on Large Language Models (LLM) and the ones that use other techniques.

 In the first one, several works based on LLM HDL code generation can be found. Some examples are DeepRTL \cite{deeprtl} o RTLCoder \cite{liu2025rtlcoder}. The second, other techniques that do not use LLMs, such as \cite{autoai2c}.

 On the architectural exploration side, there are also some other utilites such as ZigZag\cite{zigzag}.
